About MAGPIE ARTS

At Magpie Arts, we believe art should be easy, joyful, and accessible to everyone. That’s why we created PIE Kits – Paint It Easily, a unique range of DIY Paint-by-Number kits that let anyone create stunning acrylic paintings — no experience required!

With PIE Kits, you can turn cherished photos, favorite designs, or timeless sceneries into personal masterpieces that are ready to frame and treasure forever. Each kit is carefully crafted to provide not just paints and canvas, but an experience of relaxation, mindfulness, and creativity.

Mission

At MAGPIE ARTS, our mission is to make art simple, joyful, and accessible for everyone. With PIE Kits, we bring the magic of paint-by-number kits into homes—empowering beginners and hobbyists alike to create beautiful masterpieces without hesitation. Each kit is designed to inspire creativity, reduce stress, and turn precious moments into lifelong treasures on canvas.

Vision

Our vision at MAGPIE ARTS is a world where every home is filled with colors, creativity, and shared memories. We want painting to be a personal, heartfelt experience—one that families, friends, and individuals cherish and enjoy. Through PIE Kits, we inspire people to turn moments into masterpieces they can treasure for life.

  1. Select a design or upload your personal photo.
  2. Choose your kit size (Small, Medium, or Large).
